Overview

Rara National Park is one of the best tour site in Nepal. Rara Lake Tour is the best place to celebrate Holidays in Western part of Nepal. Everybody has dream to visit Majestic lake Rara, Mugu and Karnali Zone. The Rara & Mugu area is so beautiful, like a paradise. The local food with hospitality will add more memory to your visit. Rara National Park, Nepalâ€™s smallest and most scenic national park that spreads over an area of 106 sq. km is situated about 370 km north- west of Kathmandu and lies in Mugu a mountainous district of the western region of Nepal. The Rara Lake tour & National Park ascends from 1800m to 4480m forested by conifers. More than 500 different kinds of flowers and herbs including Rhododendron, the national flower, Black Juniper, Oak, West Himalayan Spruce and Himalayan Cypress are found in this Park.

Ways to travel to Rara Lake:

Via Domestic Flight: Rara Lake Tour is among one of the most popular destination in Nepal. The most easiest way to travel to Rara lake is to take a domestic flight from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj and take another domestic flight from Nepalgunj to Tancha (Mugu) airport and walk around 3 hours all the way to Rara Lake. There is a daily scheduled flight from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj however the flight from Nepalgunj to Talcha is not scheduled. It depends upon the group of travelers, seasons etc.

Activities in Rara National Park Tour

Walk to Rara lake: Major attraction of Rara National Park is the Rara lake so our guides will take you to the walk through the jungle. Horses are also available to take you to the bank of Rara lake where you will be staying.

Site Seeing: An early morning walk around the lake will make you fresh like the morning dew. The weather will be cold so one round around the lake will start your day and the apetite after the walk will feel like siting around the fire and enjoying the breakfast.

Boat ride:Once you have breakfast, you will then go for a boat ride in Rara Lake which lasts for 30 minutes. Rafting Boat will be used here and the nearby Army Camp staff will be the boat operator.

Walk to Murma top: Return back to Hotel after rafting and have lunch. Once the lunch is over, we will hike towards Murma Top which lies in the southwest part of the Lake. To reach the top of Murma, you need to hike for around 3 hours. You can enjoy the unobstructed view of the rare lake from the top. Another awesome night in Rara Lake with entertainment.
